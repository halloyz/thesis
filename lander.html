Navigating by Ear

A virtual experiment by Daniel Estehghari for the University of Copenhagen.

Welcome! In this experiment, you will get to test a brand new kind of navigation experience (in a simulated environment). Instead of looking at a map, you will follow the sound of the music to find your destination. There will be 3 navigation tasks for you to complete. Afterwards you will answer a few questions about your experience. In each of the three tasks, a different method will be used to guide you along to your destination; the goal is to evaluate which method works better, so please pay attention to how you experienced each of the three tasks! Before starting, please make sure that:


  • You are sitting comfortably at a laptop or desktop computer (not a phone/tablet).
  • You have a pair of headphones connected (any headphones will do).
  • You have approximately 30 minutes of undisturbed time to complete the experiment.

If you are ready, please input your first and last name together (no space inbetween) to move on to the first task. Your data will be handled anonymously.
